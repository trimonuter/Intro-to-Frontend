Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry

Ingredients:
  • 1 1/2 pounds sirloin steak, sliced into thin strips
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch mixed with 1 tablespoon water
  • Cooked rice, for serving (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Season the sliced steak with salt and black pepper, and toss it with 2 tablespoons of cornstarch.
  2. In a large wok or frying pan, heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil over high heat.
  3. Add the seasoned steak to the pan and stir fry for 2-3 minutes, or until browned on the outside.
  4. Remove the steak from the pan and set it aside.
  5. In the same pan, add another tablespoon of oil if needed.
  6. Add the garlic and onion to the pan and stir fry for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  7. Add the broccoli florets to the pan and stir fry for 2-3 minutes, or until they are bright green and slightly tender.
  8. Return the beef to the pan and add the beef broth, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and sugar.
  9. Bring the sauce to a boil, then reduce heat to low and let it simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  10. Stir in the cornstarch slurry to thicken the sauce.
  11. Serve the Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry hot over cooked rice, if desired.
