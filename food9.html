Lemon-Herb Grilled Pork Tenderloin

Ingredients:
  • 1 pound pork tenderloin
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried thyme
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh lemon slices, for garnish
Instructions:
  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, oregano, thyme, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Add the pork tenderloin to the bowl and turn to coat with the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for best flavor.
  3. Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Lightly oil the grates.
  4. Remove the pork tenderloin from the marinade and discard the marinade.
  5. Place the pork tenderloin on the hot grill and cook for 8-10 minutes on each side, or until the internal temperature reaches 145Â°F.
  6. Remove from the grill and let rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  7. Serve the Lemon-Herb Grilled Pork Tenderloin hot, garnished with fresh lemon slices.
