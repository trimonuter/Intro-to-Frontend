Lemon-Garlic Shrimp Skewers

Ingredients:
  • 1 1/2 pounds large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 8 wooden skewers (soaked in water for 30 minutes to prevent burning)
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges, for serving
Instructions:
  1. In a large bowl, mix together the olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Add the shrimp to the bowl and toss to coat with the marinade.
  3. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Thread the shrimp onto the skewers.
  6. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until the shrimp are pink and cooked through.
  7. Serve the shrimp skewers hot with lemon wedges.
