Pesto Chicken and Vegetable Bake

Ingredients:
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup store-bought basil pesto
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, for serving (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and black pepper.
  3. In a large baking dish, layer the chicken breasts at the bottom.
  4. Arrange the sliced red and yellow bell peppers, zucchini, yellow squash, and cherry tomatoes on top of the chicken.
  5. Spread the pesto evenly over the vegetables and chicken.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the pesto.
  7. Bake the dish in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Let the bake cool for a few minutes before serving.
  9. Serve the Pesto Chicken and Vegetable Bake hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves if desired.
