Spicy Tuna Sushi Rolls

Ingredients:
  • 2 cans of tuna, drained and flaked
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 4 sheets of nori seaweed
  • 4 cups cooked sushi rice, warm
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 small cucumber, sliced
  • Wasabi and soy sauce, for serving
Instructions:
  1. In a small bowl, mix together the tuna, mayonnaise, sriracha sauce, and soy sauce.
  2. Lay a sheet of nori seaweed, shiny side down, on a sushi mat.
  3. Spoon 1 cup of the warm sushi rice onto the nori sheet and spread it evenly, leaving a 1-inch border at the top edge.
  4. Place a few slices of avocado and cucumber on top of the rice, followed by a spoonful of the spicy tuna mixture.
  5. Roll the sushi mat tightly, pressing the ingredients together to form a tight cylinder.
  6. Repeat the process with the remaining nori sheets, rice, and fillings.
  7. Use a sharp knife to cut each roll into bite-sized pieces.
  8. Serve the Spicy Tuna Sushi Rolls with wasabi and soy sauce for dipping.
