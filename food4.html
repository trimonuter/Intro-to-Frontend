Tomato and Mozzarella Salad

Ingredients:
  • 2 pints cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. In a large bowl, combine the halved cherry tomatoes, mozzarella cheese pieces, and chopped basil leaves.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Pour the balsamic vinaigrette over the tomato and mozzarella mixture and toss to combine.
  4. Let the salad sit at room temperature for 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  5. Serve the Tomato and Mozzarella Salad chilled or at room temperature, garnished with additional basil leaves if desired.
